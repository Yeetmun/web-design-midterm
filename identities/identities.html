Common Rational Identities



Intro

These identities are useful to know when solving integrals so you can simplify them quickly if they show up or to use in combination with other methods of solving integrals. It's good to memorize these!


Rational Identities

∫exdx=ex+C ∫1xdx=lnx+C ∫1xlnbdx=logbx+C


Trig Identities

∫sinxdx=-cosx+C ∫cosxdx=sinx+C ∫tanxdx=-lncosx+C ∫secxdx=ln|secx+tanx|+C ∫sec2xdx=tanx+C ∫secxtanxdx=secx+C ∫cscxdx=ln|cscx-cotx|+C ∫csc2xdx=-cotx+C ∫secxcscxdx=lntanx+C


Inverse Trig Identities

∫sin-1xdx=xsin-1x+1-x2+C ∫cos-1xdx=xcos-1x-1-x2+C

∫tan-1xdx=xtan-1x-12ln|1+x2|+C ∫cot-1xdx=xcot-1x+12ln|1+x2|+C

∫sec-1xdx=xsec-1x-ln|x+x2+1|+C ∫csc-1xdx=xcsc-1x+ln|x+x2+1|+C
